## Positioning

**Akalynth**
*Play, build, and govern a living world.*

Akalynth lets players, builders, and operators explore a persistent fantasy world where every important action can leave evidence.

- Play through Rookguard and High City
- Explore quests, NPCs, houses, markets, and world events
- Build maps, interiors, objects, and stories
- Create NPC behavior, quests, and encounters
- Run local/offline world tools before publishing
- Preserve world changes with receipts, manifests, and replayable evidence
- Let trusted operators review, approve, and promote changes into the live world

**Gameeky** = educational no-code game creator  
**Akalynth** = persistent MMO world + creator tools + proof-backed operations

## Public Hero Draft

```text
# Akalynth

Play, build, and govern a living world.

Akalynth is a top-down fantasy MMO project with creator tooling, persistent world systems, and proof-backed operator workflows.

Players explore the world.
Builders create maps, quests, homes, NPCs, and events.
Operators promote changes through explicit review, receipts, and replayable evidence.
```

## Public Bullets

```text
Akalynth lets players and builders:

- Explore a persistent top-down fantasy world
- Start in Rookguard and travel into High City
- Create maps, interiors, NPCs, quests, and world objects
- Build player housing, markets, and social spaces
- Test world changes locally before release
- Preserve important changes with signed receipts and manifests
- Separate play, creation, moderation, and operator approval
```

```text
Play → Modify → Build → Script → Operate → Govern
```

Akalynth translation:

```text
Play the world.
```
